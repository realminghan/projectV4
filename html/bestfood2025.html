Best Street Food in Malaysia 2025

Nasi Lemak

Malaysia’s national dish served with fragrant coconut rice, sambal, fried chicken, and crunchy anchovies.

Char Kuey Teow

A smoky stir-fried noodle dish with prawns, cockles, eggs, and Chinese sausages.

Satay

Juicy skewers of marinated meat grilled over charcoal, served with peanut sauce.

Roti Canai

Fluffy and crispy flatbread served with dhal curry or spicy sambal.

Penang Assam Laksa

A tangy tamarind-based noodle soup topped with mackerel and fresh herbs.

Cendol

Shaved ice dessert with pandan jelly, coconut milk, and palm sugar syrup – a refreshing Malaysian treat.

Hokkien Mee

Rich, dark noodles stir-fried in a smoky wok with pork, prawns, and cabbage.

Rojak

A unique salad of fruits, vegetables, and fried dough fritters with sweet-spicy sauce.

Roti John

A fluffy baguette stuffed with egg, minced meat, and sauces, grilled to perfection.

Apam Balik

Crispy turnover pancake filled with peanuts, sweet corn, and sugar.

Ramly Burger

The iconic Malaysian street burger wrapped in egg with flavorful sauces.

Chicken Wings

Juicy grilled wings marinated with spices, a must-have at Malaysian pasar malam.
