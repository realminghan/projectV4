Description

Tucked away in Klang, the birthplace of bak kut teh, Kee Heong is a must-visit destination for lovers of this iconic dish. Famous for its robust herbal broth simmered for hours, the flavors strike a perfect balance between savory richness and fragrant Chinese herbs. Each bowl comes packed with tender pork ribs, tofu puffs, mushrooms, and garlic that soak up the aromatic soup beautifully. Whether paired with steamed rice or crispy fried crullers (youtiao), every spoonful delivers warmth and comfort.
