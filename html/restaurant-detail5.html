Description

Savor the refreshing taste of Malaysia with the beloved Cendol Kampung Hulu. This traditional dessert features silky green rice flour jelly served over finely shaved ice, drenched in creamy coconut milk and rich palm sugar syrup. Each spoonful delivers the perfect balance of sweetness and creaminess, with the smooth jelly adding a delightful texture and the aromatic syrup lending a deep, caramel-like flavor. A timeless treat, Cendol captures the essence of Malaysian heritage and offers a cool, satisfying escape in every bite.
